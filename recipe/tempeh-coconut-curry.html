Tempeh Coconut Curry

Ingredients

8 oz   tempeh, chopped
2 Tbsp Coconut Oil
1 Tbsp Curry Paste

Optional:
Green Onions
Minced Ginger
Chopped Zuccini
Carrots

Directions

  1. Warm up the oil in a skillet
  2. Mix the curry paste in until well-blended
  3. Add the tempeh and cook for a few minutes
  4. Add Veggies, cook for a few minutes
  5. Eat!
